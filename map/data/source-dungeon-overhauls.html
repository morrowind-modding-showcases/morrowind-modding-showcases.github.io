Darkelfguy’s List of Dungeon and Location Overhauls and Expansions:

Summary:

This is a list of all of the dungeons in vanilla Morrowind along with any and all mods that overhaul and expand them, including potentially in-development projects by various modders in the community. This is primarily meant as a modder’s resource, so people can see which dungeons have been overhauled and expanded by other modders and/or use those existing overhauls/expansions as a jumping off point for inspiration to build their own visions of those dungeons.

I will hasten to point out that just because a particular dungeon already has one or more dungeon overhauls for it, that doesn’t mean you shouldn’t also pursue your own vision for that dungeon. The beauty of modding isn’t homogeneity, it’s variety - more choice is always a good thing! You shouldn’t feel limited by what’s already been released!

That said, there are still plenty of dungeons with zero overhauls, including about 48, or over half, of Morrowind’s 90+ ancestral tombs and nearly 20 of Morrowind’s 90+ caverns. The majority of those that have been overhauled have only one existing option, so you know, there’s definitely still plenty of dungeons in need of attention!

Note: Currently this list is tied only to vanilla Morrowind and Tribunal dungeons, it doesn’t currently cover Bloodmoon. This list will be updated over time, so please keep that in mind!

Differences from Lucevar’s List:

Lucevar has already put together a fantastic resource covering dungeon mods and overhauls, which I would recommend using in addition to this list. The main differences stem primarily from a stronger focus on only dungeon overhauls and expansions. This list doesn’t cover mods that may add things to various dungeons - which is something Lucevar’s list does to some degree.

I may update this list in the future to try and cover other mods that may impact various dungeons, but given Morrowind’s prolific number of quest mods and the number of those that add one or two items to dozens of different dungeon locations around Vvardenfell, providing a comprehensive list of all mods that impact each location is a monumental undertaking, and the primary focus of this list is simply overhauls and expansions.

Additionally, this list is more up-to-date, and includes notes on potential WIP projects as posted on Discord. The reason for this is simply to give people an idea of what other projects might be out there, as well as potentially inspiration from the ideas that other modders had for these various locations.

Also, this list does NOT include mods that are no longer available - several of the mods on Lucevar’s list have been removed or hidden on the Nexus and are no longer available for public download. As such, those mods are not covered here.
