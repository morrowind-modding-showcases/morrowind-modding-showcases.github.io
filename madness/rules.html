THE RULES OF MADNESS

SEASON X · OCTOBER 2026

The tenth Madness returns this October. Registration, themes, and prizes will be proclaimed here and on the Morrowind Modding Showcases channel. Sharpen your Construction Set.

I · MUSTER

Form a team of 2 to 4 modders and register before the competition begins. Need teammates? Ask around the Morrowind Modding Community Discord — veterans regularly take first-time modders under their wing.

II · FIRST THEME

On October 1st a theme is proclaimed. Your team has two weeks to conceive, build, and release a complete mod on Nexus Mods. An entire mod. Two weeks.

III · SECOND THEME

Barely recovered, a second theme is proclaimed. Two more weeks, one more mod. Four weeks of glorious, endurance-testing insanity in total.

IV · JUDGMENT

A panel of judges scores every mod on creativity, theme, and craft. By Halloween, one team is crowned the ultimate modding champions of the year — with all the sweet prizes that entails.

WHO CAN ENTER?

Anyone who mods Morrowind — from seasoned masters to those releasing their very first mod. The Madness rewards teamwork and nerve more than raw experience.

WHAT COUNTS AS A MOD?

Houses, quests, items, NPCs, creatures — anything releasable on Nexus Mods that answers the theme. Browse nine seasons of past entries for inspiration.
