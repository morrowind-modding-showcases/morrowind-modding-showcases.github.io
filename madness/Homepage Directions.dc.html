JOIN THE MADNESS
A DECADE OF MODS
I · FORM A TEAM

Modders band together in teams of two to four. Veterans and first-timers alike — some teams are driven to conquer, others take new modders under their wing.

II · TWO THEMES, TWO MODS

A theme is proclaimed and each team has two weeks to design, build, and release a whole mod. Then it happens again. Four weeks. No mercy.

III · ONE TEAM IS CROWNED

A panel of judges scores every mod on creativity, theme, and craft. By Halloween, one team rises as the ultimate modding champions of the year.
